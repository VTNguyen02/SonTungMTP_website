Who is Sơn Tùng M-TP?

Son Tung M‑TP’s career is not only defined by record‑breaking hits and bold artistic experiments, but also by the controversies that have shaped his legacy. From plagiarism accusations and cultural debates to legal bans and critical backlash, each release has carried its own storm. Tracing these moments reveals how his music both captivates audiences and challenges boundaries, making his journey one of the most dramatic in modern Vietnamese pop.

Over the years, Sơn Tùng M-TP has released numerous chart-topping singles and albums, breaking records on streaming platforms and music charts alike. Known for his innovative music videos and memorable performances, he continues to push boundaries in V-pop while earning awards and recognition both in Vietnam and internationally.
